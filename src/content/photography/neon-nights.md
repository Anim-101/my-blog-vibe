# Neon Nights in Tokyo

Urban environments transform completely after dark. The neon signs cut through the darkness and completely alter the color palette of the city.

I wanted to capture the contrast between the solitary figures walking through the alleyways and the overwhelming scale and brightness of the advertisements towering above them.

### Technical Details

- **Camera:** Fujifilm X-T4
- **Lens:** XF 35mm f/1.4 R
- **Settings:** 35mm, f/1.4, 1/60s, ISO 1600
